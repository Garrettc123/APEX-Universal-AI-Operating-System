## ‚ö†Ô∏è Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### High-Impact Risks

#### Risk 1: Vendor Delays (Probability: 30%, Impact: High)
**Scenario:** LegalZoom or Mercury takes longer than expected

**Mitigation:**
- Have backup vendors pre-vetted
- Call vendors directly (don't just email)
- Start applications early in the day
- Consider expedited services

**Backup Plan:** Alternative incorporation services (FirstBase, Stripe Atlas)

#### Risk 2: Insufficient Capital (Probability: 15%, Impact: Critical)
**Scenario:** Don't have full $2,826 available

**Mitigation:**
- Prioritize must-haves (legal entity, EIN, bank account)
- Defer nice-to-haves (some insurance can wait 1 week)
- Use credit card with rewards
- Consider 0% APR financing

**Backup Plan:** Launch with minimum ($900: legal + domain)

#### Risk 3: No Customer Response (Probability: 40%, Impact: High)
**Scenario:** Cold emails generate no demos

**Mitigation:**
- A/B test email templates
- Personalize every email
- Multi-channel outreach (email + LinkedIn)
- Offer time-limited incentives

**Backup Plan:** Leverage network for warm intros, pivot messaging

#### Risk 4: First Customer Lost (Probability: 25%, Impact: Medium)
**Scenario:** Verbally committed customer doesn't sign

**Mitigation:**
- Get written commitments early
- Offer flexible terms
- Address objections immediately
- Have multiple prospects in pipeline

**Backup Plan:** Focus on next 3 prospects, learn from loss

### Medium-Impact Risks

#### Risk 5: Technical Issues (Probability: 35%, Impact: Medium)
**Scenario:** Website goes down or demo fails

**Mitigation:**
- Use reliable hosting (Vercel 99.99% uptime)
- Test demo environment daily
- Have backup demo recording
- Practice demo multiple times

**Backup Plan:** Screen share local environment, apologize and reschedule

#### Risk 6: Pricing Uncertainty (Probability: 50%, Impact: Medium)
**Scenario:** Not sure what to charge

**Mitigation:**
- Start with value-based pricing
- Research competitor pricing
- Test 3 price points
- Be willing to adjust

**Backup Plan:** Anchor high ($50K enterprise), negotiate down
